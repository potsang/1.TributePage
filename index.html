Michael Jackson

American singer, songwriter, record producer, dancer, and actor

Jackson (center) as a member of the Jackson 5 in 1972.

The time line of Michael Jackson's life and career:

  • 1958 - Born as Michael Joseph Jackson in Gary, Indiana
  • 1963 - Michael becomes a member of the Jackson 5 along with his brothers Tito, Jackie, Jermaine, and Marlon. He is lead singer. Their father, Joe, is their manager.
  • 1968 - The Jackson 5 sign a contract with Motown records.
  • 1969 - The Jackson 5 has their first hit single “Want You Back.” The debut single sells well over two million copies.
  • 1972 - Michael gets his first U.S. #1 solo hit with “Ben,” a song from the movie Ben.
  • 1979 - His album Off the Wall, produced by Quincy Jones, launches him to superstar standing. It sells over nineteen million copies worldwide.
  • 1980 - Wins a Grammy for Best Vocal Performance with “Don’t Stop ‘Til You Get Enough.”
  • 1982 - Releases Thriller which sells over 50 million copies worldwide and solidifies him as the King of Pop. It remains today the best selling album of all time.
  • 1984 - At the Grammy Awards, Michael and his producer Quincy Jones win record of the year for “Beat It” and Album of the Year for Thriller.
  • 1985 - Co-writes “We Are the World” with Lionel Richie.
  • 1987 - He releases the album Bad which sells over eight million copies.
  • 1992 - Founded “Heal the World Foundation”, an organization that will help improve the lives of children everywhere.
  • 1993 - A lawsuit is filed against him accusing him of seducing and abusing thirteen-year-old Jordy Chandler.
  • 1994 - Settles lawsuit by paying the plaintiff twenty million dollars.
  • 1996 - Marries Debbie Rowe in Sydney, Australia.
  • 1997 - Rowe has a baby boy, Prince Michael Joseph, Junior. His godparents are Elizabeth Taylor and Macaulay Culkin.
  • 1998 - Rowe gives birth to Paris Michael Katherine. Macaulay Culkin is the godfather.
  • 1999 - He and Debbie Rowe divorce. She gives him full custody of their two children.
  • 2001 - He is inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
  • 2002 - Prince Michael II is born. The identity of the mother is never released.
  • 2003 - He is taken into custody by the Santa Barbara County Sherriff’s Department on charges of child molestation. He posts a three million dollar bail.
  • 2005 - The jury finds Jackson not guilty.
  • 2008 - He releases Thriller 25 to mark the 25th anniversary of Thriller. It features a previously unreleased song, “For All Time,” as well as remixes with younger artists such as will.i.am and Akon.
  • 2009 - He dies of cardiac arrest due to acute propofol and benzodiazepine intoxication. The Los Angeles County Coroner rules his death a homicide. His personal physician Conrad Murray is convicted of involuntary manslaughter.

"Michael was truly something rare, the last of an endangered species. That he is the best entertainer who ever lived I will argue for life, and I come from an Elvis Presley and Motown household."
